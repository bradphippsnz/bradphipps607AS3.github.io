Tēnā koutou katoa, kei te pēhea koe?

Kia ora and welcome to the Haukai Restaurant. Nestled in the heart of the picturesque Bay of Islands, our restaurant is more than an eatery, it's a dining experience you won't want to miss. Our goal is to fill your puku/stomach with the most delicious kai/food, sourced from only the best local suppliers. Sit indoors amongst the fine artwork or seat yourself outdoors and watch the sun go down as we cook you delicious Māori and Pakeha kai with a 21st century spin.

So why not bring your whanau/family down and give us a try.

Open Wednesday to Sunday from 5pm
